ABOUT NEPALKICKS

Born in Nepal, Built for the World

Our Story

NepalKicks was born from a simple vision: to bring premium sneakers and streetwear to Nepal's vibrant urban culture. Founded in 2020 in the bustling streets of Thamel, Kathmandu, we started as a small passion project by sneaker enthusiasts who believed that style shouldn't be limited by geography.

What began as a small collection of carefully curated sneakers has grown into Nepal's premier destination for authentic streetwear and premium footwear. We understand the unique style needs of Nepal's youth - from navigating Kathmandu's diverse terrain to expressing individuality through fashion.

Every product in our collection is handpicked for quality, authenticity, and style. We work directly with authorized distributors to ensure that every pair of sneakers and every piece of apparel meets the highest standards our customers deserve.

Our Mission

To democratize access to premium streetwear and sneakers in Nepal, while celebrating and elevating local street culture.

Passion for Style

We're passionate about bringing the latest trends and timeless classics to Nepal's fashion-forward community.

Authentic Products

Every product is 100% authentic, sourced directly from authorized distributors and verified for quality.

Community First

We're more than a store - we're part of Nepal's streetwear community, supporting local culture and style.

Sustainable Future

We're committed to sustainable practices and supporting brands that care about our planet's future.
